  <h2>About Clarity Compass</h2>

  <p>As a mother of two daughters—one stepping into college, and the other entering Class 10—I’ve walked the same road many Indian parents do. From board exams and entrance tests to career confusion and sleepless nights filled with "what next?" conversations at the dinner table, I’ve been there. I know the worries. I’ve lived the questions.</p>


  <p>Clarity Compass was born out of those very moments—not as a business idea, but as a parent’s promise.</p>


  <p>With years of hands-on involvement in my children’s academic and personal growth, I began to realize how many students around us feel overwhelmed by choices, expectations, and pressure—especially when it comes to choosing a career path. I also saw how often parents feel equally lost, unsure how to support without smothering.</p>


  <p>So I decided to bridge that gap.</p>


  <p>At Clarity Compass, we guide children and teenagers to understand themselves—what motivates them, where their strengths lie, and what paths truly resonate with who they are. We work not just with the student, but with the whole family, creating clarity, confidence, and calm.</p>


  <p>Because your child’s future deserves more than guesswork—it deserves direction.</p>


  <p>Welcome to Clarity Compass. Let’s walk this journey, together.</p>
